This Is Where I Begin

I’ve spent years chasing stability — thinking once life was figured out, I’d finally get to focus on music.

I’m not here with a massive following or a million views. I’m here with one album, one voice, and the decision to stop waiting for perfect timing.

This is me starting from where I am. From zero, maybe — but finally on the right path.

If you’re reading this, maybe you’re on your own version of that path. Maybe you’re building your way out of something. If so, I hope this music gives you some light, some momentum, or at the very least — something real to hold onto for a few minutes.

More is coming.

Thank you for being here.
— PBP$
